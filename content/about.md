Fuze Shangguan is a Master's student majoring in Educational Technology (Integrated with Physics) at Jing Hengyi School of Education, Hangzhou Normal University (HZNU). He served as the Student Technical Director of the AI and Educational Robotics Laboratory at Jinghengyi School of Education, HZNU. He specializes in the design and fabrication of mechanical structures, hardware, and software for autonomous wheeled robots. His research focuses on AI-assisted Learning, the Assessment of Thinking Skills, and Educational Robotics.

上官福泽是杭州师范大学经亨颐教育学院现代教育技术(复合物理学)硕士研究生。他曾担任杭州师范大学经亨颐教育学院人工智能与教育机器人实验室技术主管(学生), 擅长设计与制造自主轮式机器人的机械结构与软硬件。他的研究聚焦于人工智能辅助学习、思维技能测评和教育机器人。

His Mottos:

*"Equipping students with future-ready skills."*

*"教会学生面向未来的能力。"*
